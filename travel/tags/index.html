30 Hours in Kumejima - the Best Island in Japan

Me and my girlfriend spent about 4 days in Okinawa as a quick getaway trip. During that time, I decided to book a flight to this super tiny island called Kumejima (久米島町), located just a 30-minute flight - or a 4-hour boat ride from the main island. We only had 30 hours there, but it was the best decision we made on this whole trip!

So we arrived to Kumejima very early in about 11 am and we had some time till our hotel check-in - we really didn’t know what to expect. We brought our snorkeling masks from home and hope for the best!
